    Sign Up and Your Account¶

    Sign Up¶

    In order to register for Bid for Game, you will need your email address, a password, and a username. The email address and username must both be unique on the site.

    1. Click on the Log in in the top right of the Bid for Game home screen. You will see the Login/Sign up form.

    2. Complete the form and click on sign up. You will receive an email to validate your identity. Click on the link in the email.

    3. Click on the Log in again, re-enter your credentials, and click on Login

    4. Complete the User name form

    5. You are now registered for Bid for Game.

    Log In¶

    If you have registered you are able to log in.

    Click on the Log in in the top right of the Bid for Game home screen. You will see the Login/Sign up form.

    Enter your username and password. If you have valid credentials you will be taken to the Mode selection screen.
