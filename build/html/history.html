    History¶

    The system remembers the last 25 boards that you have played. To access the boards click on Board selection in the Menu. You will see a screen similar to

    The North and South hands are show schematically. Select the board that you want and click on the Use selected board button and you will be presented with that board. The board number will appear in the Board source display

    Rotating boards¶

    If you want to play another hand from a board, click on the Rotate boards button.

    You may rotate the boards through 180° (rotate N seat to S), 90° clockwise (N to E) or 90° anti-clockwise (N to W).

    N.B. this affects all boards. Rotation may be carried out as many times as you wish.
