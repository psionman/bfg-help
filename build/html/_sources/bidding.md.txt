# Bidding

## Solo

You are sitting in the North seat. if you are able to bid the bidding cursor will appear under the *N* seat in the [bidding box](main_screen.html#bidding-and-auction). To make a bid click on a level and denomination in the bidding box (only valid bids appear.) You may bid until there have been three consecutive passes.

## Solo with bid comments

If you have selected *Solo with bid comments* in the [Modes screen](modes), a dialog will appear to comment on the bid and to confirm whether it is right or wrong according to the bidding engine.

![Solo bid comment](/_images/bid_comment01.png)

The display shows the bid that you made, the correct bid and whether it is right or wrong (a green tick or a red cross). A text box displays the reasoning behind the correct bid.

You are able to proceed with your bid, use the suggested bid or restart the board.

## Duo

If it is your turn to bid the bidding cursor will appear under the your seat in the [bidding box](main_screen.html#bidding-and-auction) and a yellow bar will appear under your hand. If it is not your turn to bid, the bidding box will disappear and be replaced by a message to the effect that the system is waiting for your partner to bid.

To make a bid click on a bid image in the bidding box (only valid bids appear.) You may bid until there have been three consecutive passes.

## Restart and Undo

At any stage you may restart the bidding by clicking on *Restart board* in the [Menu](menu). This will remove all bids and restart the process.

If you wish to simply undo the last bid, then click on *Undo* in the [Menu](menu).

## Three passes

When there have been three consecutive passes in the bidding, the contract will appear in the top left hand corner of the screen.

![Display contract](/_images/contract.png)

The system will then move to [cardplay](cardplay).
