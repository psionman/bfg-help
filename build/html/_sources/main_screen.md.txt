# Main Screen

The Bid for Game main screen is designed to allow you access all of the functions required to bid and play hands and at the same time to provide you with the information that you need.

![Main screen](/_images/main_screen01.png)

## Full screen

<div>The screen is best viewed using your browser's full
screen function. This can be accessed by pressing the function key <em>F11</em> or by clicking on the <em>enter full screen</em> icon: <img src="_images/full_screen_invoke.png" alt="Enter full screen" width="50"> To return to standard display, click on the function key <em>F11</em> again or click on the <em>exit full screen</em> icon <img src="_images/full_screen_remove.png" alt="Exit full screen" width="45"></div>

## Orientation

In the center of the screen is the bidding box (this will be replaced with the trick cards when you start card play.)

At the top, bottom, left and right centre are the four hands dealt for the board. At the start, only your hand is shown (at the bottom); the other three being hidden.

### Bidding and auction

In the centre of the screen is the bidding box which incorporate the auction.

![Auction and contract](/_images/bidding_box.png)

The colour of the seat abbreviations indicate the vulnerability: white for non-vulnerable, red for vulnerable. The small rectangle beneath the *N* seat indicates that the system is waiting for you (*N*) to bid.

### Contract

The contract (and vulnerability) will appear at the top left of the screen once a contract has been agreed.

### Hand visibility

At the top right of the screen is the hand visibility control.

![Hand visibility](/_images/visibility01.png)

At the moment only your hand is visible. You can choose to display

* partner's hand;
* opponents' hands;
* all hands.

If you wish to return to the standard display, click on *Hide all*.

### Scores

At the bottom left is the score display.

![Seats and scores](/_images/score01.png)

This shows your seat, the dealer and the number of tricks taken by each side during card play.

### Board source

The bottom right display shows the source of the board

![Source](/_images/source01.png)

This does not appear if the board is just a randomly dealt board, but will appear if you [select a board from your history](history) or if you use [set hands](set_hands).

## Menu

The first time that you login, the [Menu](menu) will apear on the right hand side. The menu can be hidden using the 'hamburger' button. You can choose in [Preferences](preferences) whether or not to hide the menu when not in use.
