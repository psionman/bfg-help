# Sign Up

In order to sign up for Bid for Game, you will need your email address, a username and a password. Both the password (8 characters minimum) and the username must be unique on the site.

1. From the menu bar select *My Account* and click on *Sign Up*. You will be taken to the Sign Up page.
    ![signup01](/images/signup01.jpg)

1. Enter the fields required:
   1. Username, must be unique on the site;
   2. email address, must be unique on the site;
   3. your first name; this and your last name will be visible to your partners and will help them to identify you;
   4. Password; this must be 8 characters minimum;
   5. Password confirmation; repeat your password.

1. Click on submit. I there are any errors reported, please correct them and click on submit again.

1. If there are no errors, you will see a message to say that an email has been sent to your email address.

1. Go to your email client and open the email from *support@bidforgame.com*. The body of the email will conatin a link similar to:
   
   *http://bidforgame.com/accounts/confirm-email/NTg:1l3y4F:HUNnDP-wQpTI0NG5wSZag6ysOAx84YZs4NTZgtSyHQ/*

1. Click on the link (or copy and paste into the address bar of your browser).

1. You will be shown the Confirm Email Address page

    ![confirm_email](/images/confirm_email.jpg)
    
1. Click on confirm and you will be taken to the login page. (You can login now or later).

1. You are now signed up for Bid for Game.
