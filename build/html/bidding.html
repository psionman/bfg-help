    Bidding¶

    Solo¶

    You are sitting in the North seat. if you are able to bid the bidding cursor will appear under the N seat in the bidding box. To make a bid click on a level and denomination in the bidding box (only valid bids appear.) You may bid until there have been three consecutive passes.

    Solo with bid comments¶

    If you have selected Solo with bid comments in the Modes screen, a dialog will appear to comment on the bid and to confirm whether it is right or wrong according to the bidding engine.

    The display shows the bid that you made, the correct bid and whether it is right or wrong (a green tick or a red cross). A text box displays the reasoning behind the correct bid.

    You are able to proceed with your bid, use the suggested bid or restart the board.

    Duo¶

    If it is your turn to bid the bidding cursor will appear under the your seat in the bidding box and a yellow bar will appear under your hand. If it is not your turn to bid, the bidding box will disappear and be replaced by a message to the effect that the system is waiting for your partner to bid.

    To make a bid click on a bid image in the bidding box (only valid bids appear.) You may bid until there have been three consecutive passes.

    Restart and Undo¶

    At any stage you may restart the bidding by clicking on Restart board in the Menu. This will remove all bids and restart the process.

    If you wish to simply undo the last bid, then click on Undo in the Menu.

    Three passes¶

    When there have been three consecutive passes in the bidding, the contract will appear in the top left hand corner of the screen.

    The system will then move to cardplay.
