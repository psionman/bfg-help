# History

The system remembers the last 25 boards that you have played. To access the boards click on *Board selection* in the [Menu](menu). You will see a screen similar to

![Board history](/_images/board_history01.png)

The North and South hands are show schematically. Select the board that you want and click on the *Use selected board* button and you will be presented with that board. The board number will appear in the [Board source display](main_screen.html#board-source)

![Board history source](/_images/board_source03.png)

## Rotating boards

If you want to play another hand from a board, click on the *Rotate boards* button.

![Rotate boards](/_images/rotate_boards01.png)

You may rotate the boards through 180&deg; (rotate *N* seat to *S*), 90&deg; clockwise (*N* to *E*) or 90&deg; anti-clockwise (*N* to *W*).

N.B. this affects all boards. Rotation may be carried out as many times as you wish.
