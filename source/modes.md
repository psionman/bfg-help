# Modes

Bid for Game can be used in one of three modes:

* Duo: where your bid and play with your partner;
* Solo: where you bid and play alone;
* Solo with bid comments: as Solo, but you get feedback on your bids.

To select the mode, click on the radio button in the *Select Player Mode* screen

![Select mode](/_images/mode01.png)

The system will remember the last mode that you selected.

## Duo

IF you want to play or practice with a partner, use the *Duo* mode. You will see the *Contacts* screen

![Select contact](/_images/contacts01.png)

If your partner is not shown, they must be added. Click on *Add contact*, enter their Bid for Game username and click on *Update contact*. (YOur partner must share their username with you, and your username with them.)

![Add contact](/_images/contacts02.png)

To connect to your partner click on *Connect* next to your partner's name and you will be taken to the [Main Screen](main_screen).

## Solo

Select the Solo style that you wish to use (with or without bidding comments) and you will be taken to the [Main Screen](main_screen).
