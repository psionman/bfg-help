# Buttons

To the right of the screen is a column of buttons that control your progress through the application. The buttons presented differ when bidding from those when you are playing the hand.  A button may be disabled (dimmed) if it cannot function at that stage.

## Bidding

![Bidding buttons](/_images/buttons01.png)

### New board

Deals a new board (from [Set hands](set_hands) if selected.)

### Board selection

Takes you to the [History screen](history).

### Set hands

Takes you to the [Set hands screen](set_hands).

### Restart board

Removes all actions and presents the board in its original form.

### Undo

Undoes the last action.

### Mode

Returns to the [Mode screen](modes) (the current hand will be lost but may be retrieved from [History](history)).

### Refresh

If your screen has become corrupted or does not operate when expected, then *Refresh* might help resolve the problem.

### Help

Display on-line user documentation.

## Card Play

![Card play buttons](/_images/buttons02.png)

The buttons described above retain their normal functions.

### Replay board

Return the board to the start of card play. The bidding and contract remain intact.

### Claim/Concede

Displays the [Claim/concede](cardplay.html#claim-concede) dialog.

### Previous trick

Displays the previous trick played.
