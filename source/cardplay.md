# Card play

To play a card when it it is your turn, click on the card that you select (the system will force you to follow suit if you can).

When it is you turn to play a yellow bar will appear next to the relevant hand.

![card play](/_images/play_hand01.png)

The trick cards will be displayed in the centre of the screen.

![Trick cards](/_images/played_cards01.png)

## Solo

In Solo mode you will play declarer's and and dummy's if declarer is N or S. If declarer is E or W, you will play you own hand.

The system will play E's and W's cards and S's cards if your are in Solo mode and E or W is declarer.

## Duo

In Duo mode, you will play declarer's cards and dummy's if you are declarer. If your partner is declarer the they will play declarer's and dummy's. If E or W is declarer, the you will play your hand and your partner theirs.

## Previous tricks

If you wish to see which cards were played on previous tricks, click on *Previous trick* in the [Menu](menu). You can step backwards and forwards through all of the played tricks.

![Previous trick](/_images/previous_trick01.png)

## Display auction

You may remind yourself of the auction by clicking on *Display auction* int he [Menu](menu).

## Restart, Replay and Undo

At any stage you may restart the board by clicking on *Restart board* in the [Menu](menu). This will restore all of the hands, remove all bids and restart the board.

If you click on *Replay* in the [Menu](menu), the hands are restored but the bidding and auction remain intact.

If you want to undo the last played card, click on *Undo* in the [Menu](menu).

## Makeable tricks

You may see the makeable tricks for this board by clicking on *Makeable tricks* in the [Menu](menu). (Uses a *Double-Dummy* solver.)

![Makeable tricks](/_images/makeable_tricks.png)

## Claim/Concede

If you wish to claim or concede a certain number of tricks before you have finished playing, you may click on *Claim/Concede* on the [Menu](menu). This will show the claim/concede dialog

![Claim/Concede](/_images/claim01.png)

Select the number of tricks that you wish to claim or concede. The system will check your claim and either accept

![Claim accepted](/_images/claim03.png)

or reject the claim.

![Claim rejected](/_images/claim02.png)

## Final score

When all of the cards have been played, the system will display the final score.

![Final score](/_images/final_score01.png)

If you click on *Compare score* the system will compare the score that you achieved with the score that it would have made had it played the hand. (Note this just simulates play and does is not necessarily match the score given in [Makeable tricks](makeable-tricks).)

![Compare score](/_images/final_score02.png)
