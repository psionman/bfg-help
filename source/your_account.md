# Sign Up and Your Account

## Sign Up

In order to register for Bid for Game, you will need your email address, a password, and a username. The email address and username must both be unique on the site.

1. Click on the *Log in* in the top right of the *Bid for Game* home screen. You will see the Login/Sign up form.

    ![Sign up](/_images/signup01.png)

1. Complete the form and click on sign up. You will receive an email to validate your identity. Click on the link in the email.

1. Click on the *Log in* again, re-enter your credentials, and click on *Login*

1. Complete the *User name* form

    ![User name](/_images/signup02.png)

1. You are now registered for Bid for Game.

## Log In

If you  have registered you are able to log in.

Click on the *Log in* in the top right of the *Bid for Game* home screen. You will see the Login/Sign up form.

Enter your username and password. If you have valid credentials you will be taken to the [Mode selection](modes) screen.
